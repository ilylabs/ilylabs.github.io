Aymen Mtar is a Master's student at ilylabs whose research focuses on 4D printing and smart composite materials. His work explores photoreactive bistable composite materials with potential applications in adaptive architectural and structural systems. His research contributes to the growing field of programmable matter and stimuli-responsive composites.
