Jason Gunther is a graduate student at ilylabs working on polymer processing and composite manufacturing. His research focuses on centrifugal spinning processes for the fabrication of non-woven fiber materials, with applications in filtration and respiratory protective equipment. His work builds on earlier research into non-woven fabric manufacturing and has produced novel approaches to scalable fiber production.
