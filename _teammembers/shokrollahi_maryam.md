Maryam Shokrollahi completed her PhD at the Laboratory of Ilyass Tabiai (ilylabs), co-supervised by Prof. Martine Dubé. Her research focused on the mechanical performance of parts produced by material extrusion additive manufacturing (FFF/FDM), with a particular emphasis on understanding the roles of extrudate geometry and interface bonding in driving anisotropy. Her work challenges conventional assumptions in 3D printing process design and was published in the *Journal of Manufacturing Processes* (2025).
