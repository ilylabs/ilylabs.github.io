Maryam Shokrollahi is a PhD candidate at the Laboratory of Ilyass Tabiai (ilylabs), co-supervised by Prof. Martine Dubé. Her research focuses on the mechanical performance of parts produced by material extrusion additive manufacturing (FFF/FDM), with a particular emphasis on understanding the roles of extrudate geometry and interface bonding in driving anisotropy. Her work challenges conventional assumptions in 3D printing process design and has been published in the *Journal of Manufacturing Processes* (2025).
