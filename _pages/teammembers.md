ilylabs is a research group at [École de Technologie Supérieure (ÉTS)](https://www.etsmtl.ca/) in Montréal, led by [Prof. Ilyass Tabiai](https://ilylabs.com/teammembers/tabiai_ilyass/). Our team works at the intersection of mechanics, materials science, and computational methods — developing new experimental and numerical tools for understanding how materials behave under real-world conditions.

We welcome curious, motivated students interested in composite materials, additive manufacturing, bioinspired structures, and open science. Interested in joining? [Get in touch](mailto:ilyass.tabiai@etsmtl.ca).
