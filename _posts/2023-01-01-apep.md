Apep is a story told as a text adventure that revisits fundamental ideas related to the philosophy of science through a compelling narrative. The story was written by [Hugo Labrande](https://twitter.com/hlabrande). The game is in English. Plan for roughly 6 to 7 sessions of 15–20 minutes each; you can save your progress as you go.
